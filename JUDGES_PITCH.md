- **How it works**: It treats every grid cell as a candidate for multiple types of interventions (Smog Towers, Reforestation, etc.). 
- **The Logic**: It calculates the **Gain-to-Cost ratio** for every possible unit of intervention. It accounts for **diminishing returns**‚Äîif you place a second Smog Tower in the same 200m grid, its efficiency drops. The Matrix ensures every rupee of the city budget is spent on the highest-impact location first.
